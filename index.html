About Lumina

Lumina exists to build software for organisations doing real work—without forcing them to take on the cost and complexity of building and managing software teams.

The focus is simple: design and deliver systems that hold up under real-world conditions. No templates, no off-the-shelf tools. Each platform is built around the way your organisation actually operates—whether that’s secure reporting, field coordination, or internal workflows where reliability matters.

I work with a small number of organisations where the need is clear and the systems are critical. I handle the full engineering and delivery of the system. The build itself is fully covered.

This requires direct collaboration, consistent input, and a willingness to replace fragile processes with something more dependable.

If your current systems are limiting how you operate—or you’re relying on workarounds that shouldn’t exist—let’s talk.
