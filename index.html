About Lumina

Lumina is built on a clear idea: NGOs should be able to put their resources into their mission—not into hiring a software team.

After nearly a decade building and shipping apps across iOS and Android, the commercial cycle started feeling like repetition for the sake of it. Useful, sure. Impactful? Not enough. I knew those same skills could carry far more weight in the hands of organisations working on real problems, in real communities. So I redirected that experience toward work with genuine stakes.

I build software the way these organisations work: practical, focused, and grounded in reality. No off-the-shelf kits, no cookie-cutter tools. Each system is crafted for the challenges you actually face—secure reporting platforms, field-ready safety apps, or tools that support sensitive operations where reliability is non-negotiable.

If your organisation needs software built with precision and built around your mission, I'll handle the engineering end to end, free of charge. Let's talk.
